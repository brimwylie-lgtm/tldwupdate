FAQs

Questions
answered.

What exactly does TL;DW Studios do?
We turn long-form recordings — webinars, podcasts, presentations, interviews — into polished, branded marketing assets. Using AI-assisted analysis and human editorial review, we extract your key insights and craft them into eBooks, LinkedIn posts, carousels, blog summaries, and more.
What types of content can you work from?
Almost any recorded material: YouTube videos, Zoom webinars, podcast episodes, conference talks, and interviews. If it has spoken insights, we can transform it. A link is all we need — no uploads, no transcripts, no prep work.
What do I actually receive when it's done?
Every deliverable is shaped around your objectives: what you're trying to build, who you're trying to reach, and where you're publishing. Depending on your package and goals, that can include any combination of: a branded eBook or lead magnet PDF, LinkedIn post drafts, carousel templates, a blog or newsletter summary, a transcript summary, and a content strategy overview. You tell us the goal; we build the assets that serve it.
How long does it take?
Turnaround times are based on team availability and confirmed with you before we begin. As a guide: the Insight Pack typically takes 1–2 days, the Content Amplifier 3–5 days, and the Authority Vault 5 or more days depending on volume.
Who is this for?
TL;DW is designed for speakers, podcasters, consultants, marketers, and event organizers who want to extend the life of their content and turn it into a consistent source of leads and authority. If your team isn't currently producing long-form content, this isn't the right fit.
Do you offer ongoing retainers?
Yes. Monthly retainers include LinkedIn ghostwriting (8 posts + 1 article per month) as an add-on to any package, or as a standalone service. For high-volume clients, we offer custom retainer pricing — reach out and we'll build a plan that fits.
